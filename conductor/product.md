# Product Definition: Maternity Pay Calculator

## Overview
A user-friendly Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to help expectant parents calculate and visualize their maternity pay over the course of a year, based on their pre-tax salary and specific corporate policies.

## Target Audience
- **Expecting Parents**: Looking for clear financial planning tools during their maternity leave.

## Core Features
- **Payment Timeline**: A visual breakdown of monthly payments over the leave period, providing clarity on income fluctuations.
- **Export/Share**: Ability to export calculations or share them for personal record-keeping or discussion with partners/employers.

## Product Goals
- **Accuracy & Trust**: Delivering precise calculations based on provided rules and policies to ensure users can plan with confidence.
- **Ease of Use**: A seamless, intuitive interface that simplifies complex financial calculations.

## User Experience
- **Interactive Dashboard**: A single-page application where inputs (salary, policy details) and results (timeline, totals) are updated in real-time for immediate feedback.
- **PWA Capabilities**: Fast, installable, and accessible offline for convenience.
